## Scoring Key
- Drive Lead (Q1–7): 4 choices per question → Builder (B) / Amplifier (Am) / Anchor (An) / Architect (Ar)
- Drive Follow (Q8–15): 4 choices per question → same mapping
- Amor Give (Q16–22): 5 choices per question → Expressive (E) / Devoted (D) / Intentional (I) / Present (P) / Intimate (Im)
- Amor Receive (Q23–30): 5 choices per question → same mapping
- Bridge (Q31–40): Generates compatibility narrative — not a separate type

Top score in each section = Primary type. Second highest = Secondary type (paid report).

**Four separate scores. Two potential gaps.**

Drive Gap — when Lead and Follow differ:
Example: Lead = Builder, Follow = Anchor → "You drive hard when you're in front. But when you step back, you hold the ground. Your partner may not know they have two different versions of you depending on who's steering."

Amor Gap — when Give and Receive differ:
Example: Give = Devoted, Receive = Expressive → "You show love by doing. You feel loved when someone says it. Most people in your life only know the first half."

Both gaps become key insights in the paid report.
